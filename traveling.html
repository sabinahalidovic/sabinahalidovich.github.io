Advice on traveling

  1. Research the safety of your intended destination with the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade.
  2. Carry with you at all times the contact details of the Australian embassy.
  3. For up-to-date information on 'safe' and 'unsafe' areas of the city, consult with your hotel manager or local tourist information officer.
  4. Try to blend in with the locals and avoid looking or acting like a tourist.
  5. If you are mugged, don't fight back. It is better to lose a few dollars and a wristwatch than get injured.
